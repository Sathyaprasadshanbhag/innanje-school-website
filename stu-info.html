Pledge

India is my country. All Indians are my brothers and sisters. I love my country and I am proud of its rich and varied heritage. I shall always strive to be worthy of it. I shall give due respect to my parents and my teachers and all my elders and treat everyone with courtesy. To my country and my people I pledge my devotion. In their well being and prosperity, alone lies my happiness
