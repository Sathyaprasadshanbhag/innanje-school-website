    • About S.V.S

    S.V.S English medium nursery and higher primary School Innanje was the dream venture of His Holiness Sri Vishwothama Theertha Swamiji. His vision was to empower the weaker sections of society through education, in an environment that is stimulating, challenging and grounded in values. Every child passing out of this school grows up to be commited citizen at this great country. The School aims to bring about all round development of children and in turn prepare them to meet the ever changing challenging of a globalised world. Sri Vishnumurthy Hayavadana Swami English Medium Nursery And Higher Primary School Innanje was founded in 2010. The beautiful lush green surroundings of the area inspired H.H Sri Vishwothama Theertha Swamiji of Sri Sode Vadiraja Mutt to open an English Medium Nursery and Higher Primary School Innanje. Mrs. Prabhavathi S Adiga was appointed as the Administrative officer, Mr. Pundarikaksha Mithanthaya took charge as the first Headmaster of this institution. In 2019 Mrs. Mamatha A was appointed as the Headmistress. In 2022 Mr. Anantha Moodithya was given the respensibility of managing the school as the new Administrative Officer

    Payment of School Fee

    1. The prescribed school fees is payable in full in the beginning of the academic year.
    2. The bus fees have to be paid befre 30th of every month. 3. Student will not be permitted to attend classes if there are any dues pending.
    4. Fees once paid will not be refunded.
    5. The fees structure is subject to chage periodically.
    6. Student joining the School in the middle of the academic year will have to pay tution fees for the entire year. In speical cases, it can be paid in two installment. First installment : During Admission, Second installment : First week of October

    S.V.S OBJECTIVES

    Our motto is “Tamasoma Jyothirgamaya” by giving education to the weaker sections of society. We train the students to develop leadership qualities in them. Our goal of providing education is the all-round development of the student. We encourage students to take part in community development, activities and social services
