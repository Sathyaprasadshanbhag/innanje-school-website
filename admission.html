Admissions and Withdrawals

1. Application forms are available at the School office between 10:00 a.m and 12:30 noon on working days. T.C., Conduct Certificate, Study Certificate etc. will be issued 5 days after seeing written requests for the same.

2. Transfer Certificates will be issued only on payment of dues, if any.

3. In case the student is withdrawn in the middle of a year, the tution fee and transport fees for the whole year will have to be remitted before the T.C is issued.
