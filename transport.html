Transport Services

The following rules are to be followed by the students to ensure a smooth and safe journey to and from the school. The Bus facilities are available for students on demand.

1. Maintain discipline in the bus so that the driver’s attention is not distracted.
2. Do not try to get on or off a moving bus.
3. Do not push each other when you board or alight the bus.
4. Objectives of any kind must not be dumped inside or thrown out of the buses.
5. No student should come near the door of the bus unless it has made a complete stop. Do not run to the bus, walk in a queue instead.
6. Unruly behaviour like shrieking and shouting in the bus is strictly prohibited. Student Signature Parent’s Signature
7. All the students using the school bus are excepted to be at the bus stop at least five minutes before the pick-up time of the bus. The buses will not wait for late comers.
8. Bus timings of any route may periodcially change to help the students reach quickly and to accommodate new students.
9. The bus cannot provide door to door service.
10. Students should inform the teacher or driver, if any student is not attending the school.
11. Change of bus stop for a day or a week will not be entertained.
12. Bus fee is subject to change. A late fee of Rs. 5/- per day will be collected for defaulters.

Any complaints, suggestions, comments, regarding transport should be brought to the notice of the Head of the institution immediately, So that the problem can be sorted out at the earliest.
