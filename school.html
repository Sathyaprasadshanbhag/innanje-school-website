School Activities

1. School Parliament
Student should participate in the School Parliament. SPL and Asst. SPL elected by the students are responsible persons in such parliament.

2. House Activities
In order to build a healthy competitive spirit among the students and to inculcate the habit of working in a group and develop leadership qualities, an effective house system has been introduced in the school. Each house is guided and cared by a House Master and House Misstress. Student cash participate in a number of co-curricular activities such as singing, dancing, dramatics, public speaking debating, sports and games, Bharatanatyam, karate, Abacus, Yoga etc
